Better Mobile Career Stage Visualizations

Career Stage visualizations now adapt properly to mobile screens, so you can spot breakthrough moments and career patterns whether you're at your desk or on the go.

Improvement

Breaking Artists Chart Refinement


Find artists gaining playlist traction, not just reach.


The Breaking Artists chart now ranks by playlist count growth instead of reach change, matching Breaking Tracks.

Historical views data is back on YouTube track charts. You can now track how videos performed before our current tracking window, giving you complete context for momentum shifts and growth patterns.


Try it on the Track Charts page

Dashboard Settings That Stick


Your view preferences stay put between sessions.


No more resetting your Dashboard filters every time you log in. Your Highlight, Measure Change Over, and Sort By choices now persist automatically, so you can pick up exactly where you left off.


Try it on the Dashboard
