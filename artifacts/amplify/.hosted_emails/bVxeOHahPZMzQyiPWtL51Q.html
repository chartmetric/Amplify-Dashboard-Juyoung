Improvement

Global Artist Chart Views


See how any artist performs across every country at once.


Comparing an artist's chart performance country by country used to mean endless clicking between individual markets. You'd lose track of patterns and miss the bigger picture of where momentum was really building.


The Artist Charts page now lets you filter by "All Countries" to see chart positions across every market in one view. Perfect for:


• Spotting which regions are driving an artist's global breakthrough

• Tracking how a release is performing across different territories

• Identifying emerging markets before they peak

• Building comprehensive market reports for label meetings


No more tab-switching to piece together the global story. Check it out on any Artist Page's Charts tab.
