Here's what's new in Chartmetric this month. A few highlights to help you get more from the platform.


Improvement

TikTok Chart Spike Attribution


Understand exactly why tracks are trending.


When you see a spike on the TikTok Videos chart, click to see what drove it. Get instant context on viral moments, creator features, and trending hashtags behind each surge.

Smarter Compare Tracks Search


Find the right tracks faster with better sorting and context.


Compare Tracks now sorts by Chartmetric Score so stronger tracks surface first. You'll also see release dates, track types, and tags like "most popular" in results.
